## Intro
I wanted to design a pikie plug that could be manufactured using an FDM printer.

## Requirements
- Thru Wire Harness
- Overall Weight: 1-3oz
- Floats in Saltwater
- Parametric 3D Model
- 3D Printable - mainly for prototyping
- CNC Friendly - for switching to a 'production' mode
